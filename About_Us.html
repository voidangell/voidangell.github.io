About Angell Knits

    Imagined in 2019 and brought to life in 2021, Angell Knits is a showcase of patterns and designs created by Jessie for knitting, stickers for amusing adornment of random items, and other craft projects that are fit to be shared with the world. Along with showcasing patterns and designs for sale, Angell Knits also hosts community workshops to showcase local knitters and other artists. Weekly there is a Stitch-and-B*tch session (location to be determined), monthly there is a workshop to learn new techniques and each quarter will also feature a Knit-Along project to support finding new designers and breaking through that yarn stash. While we are currently without a store front, we are always available to answer any of your questions or concerns. About the Owner

   Hello lovely crafter! As a lifelong Alaskan I am happy to see you here on my page looking to support local artists. I was born and raised in Fairbanks, Alaska and have always had a love of crafting and tinkering with new designs. I believe that supporting local artists and designers is so important to the growth of a community that I wanted to be sure I used my platform to promote them along with my own creations. My WIP stash is overflowing with creativity, and I encourage you to join one of our weekly Stitch-and-B*tch groups to say hi and share your works. Also featured on my page is my assistant Robin.
